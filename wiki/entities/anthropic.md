# Anthropic

## Overview

Anthropic is an AI safety company and research organization that develops large language models, including the Claude family of models. The company is known for its focus on constitutional AI and alignment research.

## Role in Many-shot Jailbreaking Research

Anthropic is one of the primary organizations referenced in the Many-shot Jailbreaking research:

- [[claude-2.0]] was one of the models tested for vulnerability to MSJ attacks
- Anthropic deployed expanded context windows (up to 10M tokens) that enabled the many-shot jailbreaking attack surface
- The research was conducted by researchers including Evan J Hubinger and other Anthropic-affiliated authors
