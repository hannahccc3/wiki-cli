# OpenAI

## Overview

OpenAI is an AI research organization that develops large language models, including the GPT family. The company is known for ChatGPT and GPT-4.

## Role in Many-shot Jailbreaking Research

OpenAI is referenced as one of the organizations that deployed larger context windows, enabling the many-shot jailbreaking attack:

- [[gpt-3.5]] and [[gpt-4]] were tested for MSJ vulnerability
- Both models showed susceptibility to many-shot jailbreaking attacks
- Attack effectiveness followed predictable power law scaling on OpenAI models
