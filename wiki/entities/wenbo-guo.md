# Wenbo Guo

## Overview

Wenbo Guo is a researcher at the University of California, Santa Barbara and a co-author of the RL-JACK paper on reinforcement learning-powered jailbreaking attacks against LLMs.

## Research Contributions

Guo contributed to the development of RL-JACK, particularly in the theoretical analysis of deterministic vs. stochastic search and the application of reinforcement learning to LLM security.
