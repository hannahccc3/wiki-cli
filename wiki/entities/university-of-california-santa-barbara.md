# University of California, Santa Barbara

## Overview

The University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) is a public research university located in Santa Barbara, California. It is part of the University of California system and is known for excellence in computer science and engineering research.

## Role in RL-JACK Research

Two authors of the RL-JACK paper are affiliated with UCSB:
- Yuzhou Nie
- Wenbo Guo

UCSB collaborated with Purdue University on this research project.
