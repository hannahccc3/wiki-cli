# Purdue University

## Overview

Purdue University is a public research university located in West Lafayette, Indiana. It is one of the premier engineering and computer science research institutions in the United States.

## Role in RL-JACK Research

Purdue University was one of the primary institutions involved in the development of RL-JACK. Four of the six authors are affiliated with Purdue's Computer Science department:
- Xuan Chen
- Lu Yan
- Yunshu Mao
- Xiangyu Zhang

The research was conducted in collaboration with the University of California, Santa Barbara.
