# Lu Yan

## Overview

Lu Yan is a researcher at Purdue University's Computer Science department and a co-author of the RL-JACK paper on reinforcement learning-powered jailbreaking attacks.

## Research Contributions

Yan contributed to the design and implementation of RL-JACK, particularly in developing the reinforcement learning framework for automated jailbreaking prompt generation.
