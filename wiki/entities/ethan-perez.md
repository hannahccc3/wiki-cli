# Ethan Perez

## Overview

Ethan Perez is a researcher and author of the Many-shot Jailbreaking research paper.

## Research Contributions

Ethan Perez contributed to the investigation of many-shot jailbreaking attacks on large language models. His work focused on understanding the vulnerability of LLMs to long-context attacks and potential mitigation strategies.
