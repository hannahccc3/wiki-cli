# Guo et al.

## Overview

**Guo et al.** are the authors of the paper "JAILBREAK-R1: Exploring the Jailbreak Capabilities of LLMs via Reinforcement Learning" published in 2025.

## Contribution

The research team developed JAILBREAK-R1, a novel automated red teaming training framework that combines reinforcement learning with multi-stage training to generate diverse and effective jailbreak attack prompts.
