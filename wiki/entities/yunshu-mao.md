# Yunshu Mao

## Overview

Yunshu Mao is a researcher affiliated with Purdue University's Computer Science department and a co-author of the RL-JACK paper.

## Research Contributions

Mao contributed to the research on using deep reinforcement learning for black-box jailbreaking attacks, with a focus on action space design and reward function optimization.
