# Cem Anil

## Overview

Cem Anil is a researcher and one of the primary authors of the Many-shot Jailbreaking research paper published in 2024.

## Research Contributions

Cem Anil contributed to the investigation of long-context attacks on large language models, specifically:

- Studying the effectiveness of many-shot jailbreaking across multiple models
- Analyzing power law scaling relationships in MSJ attacks
- Evaluating mitigation strategies including supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning
