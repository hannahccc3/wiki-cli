# Google DeepMind

## Overview

Google DeepMind is an AI research division of Google that focuses on advancing AI capabilities and safety research.

## Role in Many-shot Jailbreaking Research

Google DeepMind is referenced as one of the organizations that deployed expanded context windows (up to 10M tokens), creating the attack surface for many-shot jailbreaking. Their models are part of the broader ecosystem vulnerable to MSJ attacks.
