# Xuan Chen

## Overview

Xuan Chen is a researcher affiliated with Purdue University's Computer Science department. He is one of the primary authors of the RL-JACK paper on reinforcement learning-powered jailbreaking attacks against large language models.

## Research Contributions

Chen contributed to the development of RL-JACK, which represents a novel approach to black-box jailbreaking using deep reinforcement learning. His work focuses on LLM security and adversarial machine learning.
