# Yuzhou Nie

## Overview

Yuzhou Nie is a researcher affiliated with the University of California, Santa Barbara. He is a co-author of the RL-JACK paper and has research interests spanning LLM security and machine learning.

## Research Contributions

Nie contributed to the development of RL-JACK, focusing on the application of reinforcement learning to jailbreaking attacks against large language models. His research bridges work between UCSB and Purdue University.
