# Samuel R. Bowman

## Overview

Samuel R. Bowman is a researcher and co-author of the Many-shot Jailbreaking paper (2024). He is well-known for his work in natural language processing, particularly regarding large language model capabilities and alignment.

## Research Focus

Bowman's contributions to the paper involved understanding the fundamental mechanisms underlying in-context learning and how these relate to jailbreaking vulnerabilities.
